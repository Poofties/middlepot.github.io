　potetomid music　


my musical tastes has a lot of variety, i like a lot of different genres and some of them are a little unknown. it has always been a bit complicated for me to explain what kind of music i like for people who i just met, so i had an idea : why not organize all my tastes with playlists ? and it was a great idea, playlists are so fun !
i started making playlists on 8tracks, but the site had some changes that prevented me from listening to playlists like before, since i'm not from the region that they prioritized. i don't support this kind of prioritization between regions, so i decided to use youtube to make new playlists.
i love to share new music with people, share the work of artists who do a fantastic job is something i really love to do. not all artists are appropriately recognized, and this is very sad.
besides, it's a lot of fun to discover new things, isn't it ? sometimes this can be a magical experience !
so in my free time, i'm making playlists. do you enjoy making playlists too ? share with me !
