　moeP　


this project is a little stuck because my intentions with it require a lot of time and patience, something that i have lacked lately ...
my intentions was to make animated pvs for songs that i like with mini stories and characters. there are so many songs that seem to tell stories, i always imagine incredible things with them !
however, i'm going to take a long time to get my imagination on video, don't you think ? so i've decided for now to just combine the songs i like and make compilations and remixes. a few nice things came out in this process and i hope you like them !
so, i have specifically four ocs. their personalities will not be as specific as they would vary according to the story. i don't even know their names ! they have only been chosen and are trapped in a strange reality, they didn't know each other until then and they have to live different situations. they get too involved in things they don't understand. is it all illusion ? so why can't they leave ? why do they look so much alike ?
let's get to know them with this quick introduction :



　　　　　　　　1　　

　　　　　　she's sentimental and warm.
　　　　　　cries a lot.
　　　　　　super shy, not very brave.
　　　　　　everything has to be peaceful !
　　　　　　from time to time she gives a touch of softness.



　　　　　　　　2　　

　　　　　　she's kinda egocentric.
　　　　　　she thinks she's the smartest.
　　　　　　acts questionably.
　　　　　　really not afraid of anything !
　　　　　　from time to time she gives a touch of bitterness.



　　　　　　　　3　　

　　　　　　she's super happy and excited !!
　　　　　　doesn't understand many things.
　　　　　　always puts herself in danger.
　　　　　　love love love !!!
　　　　　　from time to time she gives a touch of sweetness.



　　　　　　　　4　　

　　　　　　she doesn't care.
　　　　　　where are her glasses ?
　　　　　　don't think fast, but think very well.
　　　　　　loves insects.
　　　　　　from time to time she gives a touch of adjustment.



※ i like to use flandre scarlet in my moeP accounts because her cute + violent personality is well compatible with the music style i would like to use in this project !
