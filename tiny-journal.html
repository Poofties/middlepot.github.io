☆ potato being ☆


　　"Now you're a potato"
　　Our story begins with that phrase. And what does it mean? I don't know.
　　Let's try to understand together. At the end of the year everyone waits for 00:00 (midnight, if you don't know), to become simple and insignificant potatoes. How insignificant? Potatoes are also people!
　　In fact, potatoes require their potato rights! And what are these? I don't know.
　　Potatoes want the honest and straightforward right to be potatoes, but not any potatoes, they want to be you! That explains the sentence with which we begin our text.
　　But wait there, how do potatoes want to be us? I don't know.
　　Potatoes, not having their own rights, want our rights, of being potatoes. And what are our rights? I don't know.
　　How much do potatoes want from us? What rights do they seek in us? I don't know, and I don't know.
　　Or maybe I know; all they want is to have the right to taste us, just like we do with them!
　　"Now you're a potato", and what will you do? Savor a being potato!

☆ i want to be comfortable ☆


　　the only thing i really want is to feel comfortable constantly. i don't want to feel that i have to justify everything i do or cry for the mistakes of others. i don't want to feel like a bad girl when i don't reach unattainable expectations.
　　i want to feel comfortable being alive and in my body. i want to feel that everything is fine and that i'm safe all the time. i want to know that i'm perfect with my imperfections. i want to live fully and without regrets.
　　i want to realize that nothing can hurt me more than myself.

☆ the probably blind little girl ☆


　　Once upon a time there was this girl, just like any girl we don't know, or we don't know if they really exist. It is not known until today how or if she really went blind, but let's just proceed.
　　One easter morning she had not yet got out of bed, or at least opened the eyes, so the easter buny came and called her to eat some chocolate, just to relax, because on that day there was a beautiful easter morning, despite to be christmas.
　　Then she awoke, still a little sleepy, continued with her eyes closed. The buny said:
　　— Hey, little girl, open your eyes! Don't you see that today is a special day?
　　Then slowly she opened her eyes, that were still closed, when she saw the buny and realized that she was blind, although her eyes remained closed, she realized that she saw nothing.
　　The buny was sad, but it was not because of this. He was not very happy with his new job, he used to be a goblin who helped little girls get out of bed and realize they were not blind, just kept their eyes closed.
　　This story may or may not make some sense, in fact, everything was confused because the little christmas bunny thought it was easter. The girl couldn't see because her eyes were closed, maybe she was even afraid of the bunny because he wasn't the easter bunny.
　　Moral of the story: To every five people, the double is ten. In addition, buny is not supposed to be bunny, it's the name of the bunny, but it's not written as a name, because it's a bunny name.
